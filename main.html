Flowers photos

Flowers are unarguably the most ornamental parts of any flowering plant. Flowers also carry huge sentimental and emotional value. That explains why they’re often given as presents to symbolize a range of emotions, including love, fidelity, and empathy.Like various other plant parts, flowers also boast therapeutic and aromatherapy properties. Certain flowers like the lotus also have a deep cultural and religious significance.
